Chicken Curry

If you love Indian food you have to try this recipe! This Curry tastes just as good as what you’d get at an Indian restaurant, if not better, and it’s easy to make.

Brimming with flavor thanks to all the spices, it’s rich and creamy and it’s amazingly hearty when you finish it off with basmati rice or naan bread.


Ingredients:

  • 200g rice
  • 150g chicken
  • 30g peanut butter
  • 100ml coconut milk
  • Sunflower oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cayenne Pepper

Steps:

  1. Boil rice at low temperature for 30min
  2. Heat Sunflower oil and fry the chicken
  3. Add peanut butter and coconut milk to the chicken. Mix them until you receive a cream
  4. Add spices to your liking
  5. Serve & enjoy
